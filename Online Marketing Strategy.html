Online Marketing Strategy

Role

I am fully in charge of online marketing strategy in my current company. I cooperate with a front-end developer and a grapic designer.

Duration

2 years, Oct 2016 - Dec 2018

Product Feature

USA SIM cards for Taiwanese travelers who intend to visit the US

Analysis

Market Analysis: SWOT
Strength

- Official SIM cards, not MVNO
- Flexible options for different stay period
- Free shipping and airport pick-up service
- Customer Service in Chinese

Weakness

- Too many products to choose
- Without brick-and-mortar stores

Opportunity

- Increasing numbers of back-packing travelers
- Increasing numbers of online-shopping customers

Threat

- New competitors
- Brand awareness

The benefits of our services are addressed to encourage customers' confidence toward our prodcuts.
